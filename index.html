We Are Stellar!

Stellar is a community, non-profit organization that is made up by a family of FIRST Robotics teams ranging from kindergaten all the way though highschool.

Building robots and growing people!

Our Organization has been serving Richland county and surrounding counties for over 8 years! Our programs aim promote and equip students with essential STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) skills.

Powered by our community!

Stellar is 100% volunteer dependent. Our funding comes from sponsorships, donations, fund-raisers and grants.
